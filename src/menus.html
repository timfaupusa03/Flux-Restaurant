"Between Bites: A Journey of Tension"

Savor nine thoughtfully crafted courses, each exploring a core relational dialectic — the beautiful push and pull of connection and contradiction. This tasting menu invites you to taste the complexities of relationships through flavor, texture, and presentation.Savor nine thoughtfully crafted courses, each exploring a core relational dialectic — the beautiful push and pull of connection and contradiction. This tasting menu invites you to taste the complexities of relationships through flavor, texture, and presentation.

1 - Gravity / Flight

Tension: Autonomy ↔ Connection

Seared scallop atop a delicate nest of crispy kataifi pastry, surrounded by a silky leek and champagne velouté.

Representation: The scallop (center) represents individuality, distinct and self-contained, while the airy pastry nest connects it to the surrounding sauce — symbolizing the push and pull between independence and togetherness.

2 - Glass / Curtain

Tension: Openness ↔ Closedness

A crystal-clear consommé poured tableside over a hidden dumpling filled with black garlic and mushroom confit.

Representation: The transparent broth invites openness — you can see through it — yet the dumpling conceals its contents until eaten, reflecting the balance between revealing and keeping things private.

3 - Clockwork / Firework

Tension: Predictability ↔ Novelty

A perfectly layered potato mille-feuille served with a surprise seasonal foam that changes daily.

Representation: The mille-feuille is precise and consistent, while the ever-changing foam introduces unpredictability, highlighting stability alongside spontaneous excitement.

4 - Lantern in the Forest

Tension: Inclusion ↔ Seclusion

A shared board for two, with cured meats and pickled vegetables placed around a central dish of warm, spiced lentils.

Representation: The communal elements encourage inclusion and togetherness, but the central dish — eaten with individual spoons — symbolizes moments of personal retreat.

5 - Stage Whisper

Tension: Revelation ↔ Concealment

A dome-covered plate unveiled at the table, revealing smoked trout with horseradish cream and beetroot gelée.

Representation: The dramatic reveal mirrors sharing something publicly, while the hidden presentation under the dome nods to secrets kept until the right moment.

6 - Bitten Tongue

Tension: Expression ↔ Nonexpression

Chili-oil-poached prawn on a bed of cooling cucumber and mint salad.

Representation: The heat of the chili symbolizes unrestrained expression, while the cooling elements represent restraint and composure — a literal hot-and-cool interplay.

7 - Mirror / Canvas

Tension: Ideal ↔ Real

A glossy, flawless chocolate mirror-glaze exterior encasing a rustic crumble and tart berry compote.

Representation: The perfect outer layer represents expectations and ideals, while the textured, imperfect interior reflects the raw truth of reality.

8 - Roots / Horizons

Tension: Past ↔ Future

Heritage grain risotto with modernist plating — microgreens, edible flowers, and a smoked citrus air.

Representation: The ancient grains anchor the dish in tradition (past), while the avant-garde presentation and techniques push it toward the future.

9 - Sun / Rain

Tension: Joy ↔ Sorrow

Lemon posset with a streak of salted caramel and candied violet petals.

Representation: The bright, uplifting citrus represents joy, while the bittersweet caramel mirrors life's inevitable sorrows — coexisting in a single spoonful.

"Contours & Contrasts: Wines in Dialogue"

A curated selection of wines designed to mirror and enhance each course's tension. From bright bubbles to rich sweetness, these pours create a dynamic conversation on your palate, balancing harmony with contrast.

1 - Champagne Brut

Bright bubbles echo the dish's lightness and contrast.

2 - Albariño

Fresh and crisp, it matches the clear broth's purity.

3 - Chardonnay (unoaked)

Clean and precise, balancing predictability with surprise.

4 - Grenache Blend

Juicy and spicy, perfect for shared and solo flavors.

5 - Riesling Kabinett

Sweet and sharp to balance smoky and bold notes.

6 - Gewürztraminer

Aromatic sweetness cools spicy heat.

7 - Tawny Port

Rich and nutty, pairs with chocolate and berries.

8 - Orange Wine

Earthy and vibrant, bridging tradition and innovation.

9 - Late Harvest Sauvignon Blanc

Lush sweetness contrasts with bright acidity.

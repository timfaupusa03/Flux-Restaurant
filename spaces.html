SPACES & FINE DINING

Welcome to the Architecture of Tension

At Flux, every dining space is thoughtfully designed to immerse you in the rhythms of relationship — the balances and contradictions that shape how we connect, separate, reveal, and retreat. Each room offers a unique setting for your shared journey through relational dialectics.

THE CONFLUENCE HALL

The Confluence Hall embodies the tension of Autonomy ↔ Connection — a space where individuality and togetherness flow side by side.
Warm wood tones blend with sleek steel accents. Tables are arranged asymmetrically to foster intimate pockets of space, while light cascades through layered glass panels that reflect and distort, echoing the fluid dance of closeness and independence.
Here, pairs and small groups can engage deeply while savoring their own rhythms — part of the community, yet distinct within it.

For 2 to 6 guests

THE HORIZON GARDEN

Step into the Horizon Garden, where Stability ↔ Change comes alive. Rooted in nature's steady rhythms yet shifting with light, season, and weather, this space mirrors the comfort of the familiar and the thrill of growth. Modular seating lets you dine alone, share a table for 15, or anywhere in between — your arrangement reflects the balance between constancy and transformation. Maple wood tables, vibrant greenery, bright canopy drapes and seasonal blooms make the Horizon Garden a living metaphor for relationships in motion.

Outdoor area for 2 to 8 guests

THE EMBER ALCOVE

The Ember Alcove embodies the tension of Openness ↔ Closedness — the delicate balance between sharing and protecting. The sweeping curves and layered textures form a warm, protective shell, offering seclusion and intimacy. Yet, the subtle openness of the space, with glimpses into the surrounding restaurant, keeps guests connected to the wider rhythm of the evening. An exclusive feature of The Ember Alcove is its Chef's Passage — a private corridor leading guests behind the scenes for a curated kitchen tour. In this moment, the walls of privacy fall away as the chef reveals the inspirations, ingredients, and stories behind the evening's menu, before diners retreat once more into the intimate glow of their alcove.

Private dining for 2 to 4 guests
